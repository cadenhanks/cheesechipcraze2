How-To Guide for the Gourmet Cooks

If You Don't Want to Get So Fancy Here are a couple quick and easy cheese chip recipes

  1. Microwave Recipe (Feeds 1-2 People)
      2. What You Need
    • Microwave (Duh)
    • Your Favorite Tortilla Chips
    • Shredded Cheese (Can Be Pre-Shredded or You Can Shred It Yourself)
    • Optional Extra Ingredients: Bacon Bits, Doritos, Sliced Jalepeños,
      • How To make
    • Spread Chips on a Plate
    • Cover the Chips Completely in Shredded Cheese and Any Other Desired Toppings
    • Microwave for 30 Seconds
    • Enjoy with some Salsa (Mix Salsa with Sour Cream for Extra Creamy Salsa)
  2. Oven Recipe (Feeds a Lot More People)
      3. What You Need
    • Oven (Duh Again, I Guess)
    • Sheet Pan
    • Tinfoil
    • Your Favorite Tortilla Chips
    • Shredded Cheese (Can Be Pre-Shredded or You Can Shred It Yourself)
    • Optional Extra Ingredients: Bacon Bits, Doritos, Sliced Jalepeños,
      • How to Make
    • Cover Sheet Pan in Tinfoil
    • Cover Tinfoil in Chips
    • Cover Chips in Cheese and Other Desired Toppings
    • Cook for 2-3 Minutes at 400˚F
    • Broil for 30 seconds for some extra crisp
    • Enjoy!
